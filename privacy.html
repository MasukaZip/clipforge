Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

ClipForge Publisher is designed to run locally on the user's computer. Video files are processed locally unless the user explicitly chooses to upload a finished clip to a connected platform.

Information Used

  • Local video paths and export settings selected by the user.
  • Captions, hashtags and cover information generated for the user's clips.
  • TikTok authorization tokens when the user connects a TikTok account for upload features.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to process clips, generate publishing assets and upload selected videos through official platform APIs. ClipForge Publisher does not sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

TikTok upload features use TikTok's official APIs. When using those features, TikTok's own terms and privacy policies also apply.

Data Storage

App settings may be stored locally on the user's computer. The user can remove local configuration files at any time.
