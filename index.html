ClipForge Publisher

ClipForge Publisher is a desktop tool for creators who prepare short vertical MP4 videos for TikTok, YouTube Shorts and similar platforms.

The app helps the user export clips with audio, burned-in subtitles, covers, captions and hashtags. TikTok upload features are used only by the authorized account owner for videos selected inside the app.

If you arrived here after authorizing TikTok, keep ClipForge open locally and wait a moment. The authorization code will be forwarded to the local app automatically.
